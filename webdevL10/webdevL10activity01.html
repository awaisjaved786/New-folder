Cricket is a popular bat-and-ball team sport played by two teams of 11 players on an oval field with a rectangular pitch at its center. The objective is to score more runs than the opposing team by hitting a ball with a bat and running between two wickets, while the fielding team tries to dismiss the batters by taking wickets or catching the ball!

Originating in England, it is now a major sport in countries like India, Pakistan, and Australia..
